# HR Interviewer Video

Place your HR interview video file here as:
`hr-interviewer.mp4`

## Requirements:
- Format: MP4 (H.264)
- Duration: 30–120 seconds (loops automatically)
- Content: Professional HR person sitting at a desk, speaking/listening
- Resolution: 1280x720 minimum recommended

## Free Video Sources:
- Pexels.com (search "business woman interview" or "HR professional")
- Pixabay.com
- Videvo.net

## Usage:
The video is referenced in the interview page as:
```html
<video autoplay loop muted playsinline>
  <source src="/videos/hr-interviewer.mp4" type="video/mp4">
</video>
```

If no video is found, the app will show a professional gradient background with an HR avatar emoji as fallback.
